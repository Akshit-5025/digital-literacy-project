This infographic explains the concept of **digital literacy** and its importance in today’s world. It defines digital literacy as the ability to effectively, responsibly, and safely find, evaluate, use, and create information using digital technologies. The infographic highlights key components such as basic technical skills, evaluating information, online communication, and digital safety. It also emphasizes why digital literacy matters—improving academic performance, ensuring online safety, supporting career success, promoting responsible behavior, and developing critical thinking to identify misinformation. Overall, it encourages individuals to stay smart, safe, and responsible online.
